    Jockey Conference Center (JCC) facilitates your every need with our excellent guidance and assistance in terms of technical and operational support to make your dream events come true.

    Located in the heart of Jakarta, Jockey Conference Center (JCC) first opened its doors in 1974. Being one of the earliest convention centers, JCC had the chance to witness many historical events.

    In its journey, Jockey Conference Center (JCC) has held various distinguished national and international events, including the 11th Non-Aligned Movement and Asian-African Conference.
